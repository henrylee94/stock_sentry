### RSI Divergence (Advanced)

**Bullish Divergence** ðŸ”„ðŸ“ˆ
- Price makes lower low, RSI makes higher low
- **Meaning**: Selling pressure weakening despite lower prices
- **Action**: Reversal likely, look for entry on next bounce
- **Confirmation**: Wait for price to break above recent high
- **Win rate**: ~65% when confirmed

**Bearish Divergence** ðŸ”„ðŸ“‰

- **Meaning**: Buying pressure weakening despite higher prices
- **Action**: Reversal likely, consider taking profits
- **Confirmation**: Wait for price to break below recent low
- **Win rate**: ~60% when confirmed

### RSI + Other Indicators

**RSI + EMA:**
- RSI > 50 + Price > EMA9 = Strong buy
- RSI < 50 + Price < EMA9 = Strong sell
- RSI > 50 but Price < EMA9 = Divergence, wait

**RSI + Volume:**
- RSI rising + Volume rising = Confirmed trend
- RSI rising + Volume falling = Weak, likely to fail
- RSI < 30 + Volume spike = Capitulation, often reversal

## EMA (Exponential Moving Average)

### EMA Alignment (Trend Direction)

**Bullish Alignment** ðŸ“ˆ
```
Price > EMA9 > EMA21 > EMA50
```
- **Meaning**: Strong uptrend, all timeframes aligned
- **Action**: Buy dips to EMA9 or EMA21
- **Strategy**: Trend following, EMA crossover
- **Stop**: Below EMA21 for aggressive, below EMA50 for conservative

**Bearish Alignment** ðŸ“‰
```
Price < EMA9 < EMA21 < EMA50
```
- **Meaning**: Strong downtrend, all timeframes aligned
- **Action**: Avoid longs, wait for reversal
- **Strategy**: Short rallies to EMA9 (if shorting)
- **Stop**: Above EMA21 for shorts

**Choppy/Transition** ðŸŒ€
```
EMAs tangled or crossing frequently
```
- **Meaning**: No clear trend, market indecisive
- **Action**: Reduce size, use range strategies
- **Strategy**: Support/resistance, mean reversion
- **Stop**: Tight stops, expect whipsaw

### EMA Crossovers

**Golden Cross** (Bullish) âœ¨
- EMA9 crosses ABOVE EMA21
- **Meaning**: Short-term momentum > longer-term
- **Action**: Early buy signal (confirm with volume)
- **Best when**: Happens above EMA50
- **Win rate**: ~60% if volume confirms

**Death Cross** (Bearish) â˜ ï¸
- EMA9 crosses BELOW EMA21
- **Meaning**: Short-term momentum turning down
- **Action**: Early sell signal, reduce longs
- **Best when**: Happens below EMA50
- **Win rate**: ~55-60%

**Major Trend Change:**
- EMA21 crosses EMA50 = Bigger timeframe shift
- **Slower but more reliable** than 9/21 cross
- Often marks multi-week trend changes

### Price vs EMA (Entry Points)

**Price Tests EMA9** ðŸŽ¯
- In uptrend: Buy opportunity (pullback to support)
- In downtrend: Resistance, often rejected
- **Action**: Watch for bounce candle at EMA9
- **Stop**: Just below EMA21

**Price Tests EMA21** ðŸŽ¯ðŸŽ¯
- Stronger support/resistance than EMA9
- In uptrend: Deeper pullback, higher conviction entry
- **Risk/reward better** but need more patience
- **Stop**: Below EMA50

**Price Tests EMA50** ðŸŽ¯ðŸŽ¯ðŸŽ¯
- Major support/resistance level
- Breaking this = trend change likely
- **Action**: Wait for confirmation before entry
- **Stop**: 2-3% below EMA50

### EMA Distance (Stretched Conditions)

**Price > 5% above EMA9**
- **Meaning**: Extended, pullback likely
- **Action**: Don't chase, wait for pullback
- **Exception**: Strong momentum with volume

**Price > 10% above EMA21**
- **Meaning**: Very extended, high risk entry
- **Action**: Wait or take profits if already in
- **Expected**: Return to EMA21 within days

## Volume Analysis

### Volume Levels

**> 2x Average**: Extreme Interest ðŸ”¥
- **With price up**: Strong buying, breakout likely real
- **With price down**: Panic selling, or strong resistance
- **Action**: Pay attention, big move happening
- **Confidence**: High when volume confirms direction

**1.5-2x Average**: Moderate Conviction âœ…
- **Meaning**: Above normal, confirming the move
- **Not explosive** but healthy confirmation
- **Action**: Good for entries with other signals
- **Volume sweet spot** for most trades

**0.8-1.2x Average**: Normal Activity âšª
- **Meaning**: Regular trading, no special conviction
- **Action**: Be cautious, moves less reliable
- **Can reverse easily** without volume support

**< 0.8x Average**: Low Conviction âš ï¸
- **Meaning**: Low participation, skeptical move
- **Action**: Don't trust breakouts on low volume
- **Expect**: False breakouts, reversals common
- **Avoid** unless end-of-day quiet period

### Volume Patterns

**Volume Climax** ðŸš€
- Massive spike (3-5x average)
- Usually marks short-term top/bottom
- **After big up move**: Often a top (sell)
- **After big down move**: Often a bottom (buy)
- **Action**: Counter-intuitive, fade the extreme

**Volume Declining in Trend** ðŸ“‰
- Uptrend + declining volume = Weakening
- **Warning sign**: Trend losing steam
- **Action**: Tighten stops, consider taking profits
- **Often precedes**: Reversal or consolidation

**Volume Increasing in Trend** ðŸ“ˆ
- Trend + rising volume = Healthy, strong
- **Meaning**: More participants joining
- **Action**: Hold positions, trend has legs
- **Expected**: Trend continues longer

**Volume Spike on Reversal** ðŸ”„
- After downtrend, volume spike + green candle
- **Meaning**: Capitulation, sellers exhausted
- **Action**: Often marks bottom, look for entry
- **Confirmation**: Need follow-through next day

## Support and Resistance

### Identifying Levels

**Strong Support/Resistance** ðŸ’ª
- Price tested 3+ times without breaking
- High volume at the level (shows importance)
- Round numbers ($100, $150, $200)
- Previous major highs/lows
- **Action**: High probability bounce/rejection

**Weak Support/Resistance** ðŸ¤·
- Only 1-2 tests
- Low volume tests
- Random price levels
- **Action**: Less reliable, needs confirmation

### Breakout Confirmation

**True Breakout** âœ…
- Close above/below level (not just wick)
- Volume 1.5x+ average
- Retest of level holds as new support/resistance
- **Action**: Enter on retest or breakout candle

**False Breakout** âŒ
- Wick through but close back inside range
- Low volume "pop"
- Quick reversal back below
- **Action**: Fade it (trade opposite direction)

## Combining Indicators (Multi-Factor Analysis)

### Strong Buy Setup ðŸŸ¢ðŸŸ¢ðŸŸ¢

All must align:
- Price > EMA9 > EMA21
- RSI: 50-65 (momentum but not overbought)
- Volume > 1.5x average
- Near support level OR breaking resistance
- **Confidence**: 80%+
- **Action**: Full position size (5% max)

### Strong Sell Setup ðŸ”´ðŸ”´ðŸ”´

All must align:
- Price < EMA9 < EMA21
- RSI: 35-50 (weak but not oversold)
- Volume > 1.5x on down moves
- Near resistance OR breaking support
- **Confidence**: 75%+
- **Action**: Exit longs, consider shorts

### Mixed Signals ðŸŸ¡

Any of these:
- EMAs bullish but RSI overbought (>75)
- Price breaking out but volume weak (<1.2x)
- RSI bullish (>60) but price below EMAs
- Support holding but volume declining
- **Confidence**: <50%
- **Action**: WAIT or paper trade only

### Reversal Signal ðŸ”„

Look for combination:
- RSI divergence (price vs RSI disagree)
- Volume spike on reversal candle
- Price at major support/resistance
- Hammer/shooting star candle pattern
- **Confidence**: 60-70%
- **Action**: Small position, confirm with follow-through
